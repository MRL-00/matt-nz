My loose rules for being useful as an architect, ship a working slice before the diagram, write the ADR after the prototype, and never use the word "synergy".

## Working slice before diagram

The fastest way to lose credibility with an engineering team is to show up to the second meeting with a diagram and no code. I have done this. I have watched other people do this. It does not get better the more senior you get.
