I started writing C# in 2011. Since then I've been paid to write five other languages full-time. The thing that has changed the least is also the thing nobody markets — how you think about problems.

## The languages, briefly

C#, then Objective-C, then Swift, then a stretch of Kotlin, then Flutter (Dart), then Go, then a lot of TypeScript on the side. The order matters less than you'd think.
