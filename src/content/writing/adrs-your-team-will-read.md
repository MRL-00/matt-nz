Most architecture decision records die because they answer questions nobody asked.

## The template

Four sections. Always the same four. In this order:

- **What we're deciding.** One sentence. The first sentence of a reply if someone asked you in Slack.
- **What we considered.** Bullets, not paragraphs. Two to four options, with one or two lines each.
- **What we picked, and why.** The reasoning. Be honest about trade-offs.
- **What we'd want to revisit.** When this decision should be reopened, and what would trigger it.

That's it. Anything more is a design doc, not an ADR.
